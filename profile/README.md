# College Station Computer Science

College Station Computer Science (CSCS) is a community hub for tech enthusiasts, developers, and learners in the Bryan-College Station area. Our goal is to provide a centralized space for the local tech community to connect, learn, and grow together.

## What We Do

*   **Book Club:** We host a book club to discuss technical books, covering everything from software engineering classics to the latest releases.
*   **LeetCode Meetups:** Join us for collaborative coding sessions to practice for interviews, sharpen your algorithm skills, and solve interesting problems.
*   **Technical Blogs:** We provide a platform for community members to read and write technical blog posts on a wide range of topics.
*   **Community Events:** We organize workshops, tech talks, and networking events to bring the local tech community together.
